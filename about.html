Our story

Dallas's Trusted Electrical Contractor Since 2008

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services that exceed customer expectations.

Electrics Contractor Pros was founded by master electrician John Davis, who saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in Dallas. Starting with a single truck, John built a reputation for integrity and craftsmanship. Today, we're a team of 15 licensed electricians serving thousands of satisfied customers.

We believe in continuous training, using the latest tools, and treating every home like our own. Our 5.0-star rating reflects our commitment to excellence. Whether it's a small repair or a large commercial project, we bring the same dedication to every job.
