Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Electricians

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services with a focus on customer satisfaction and craftsmanship.

Founded in 2009, Electrics Contractor Pros started as a small family operation serving Dallas neighborhoods. Our founder, a master electrician, saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in a market full of inconsistent contractors. We built our reputation one job at a time, emphasizing transparency and attention to detail.

Today, we serve hundreds of residential and commercial clients across Dallas. Our team continues to grow, but our values remain the same: treat every home like our own, communicate clearly, and never cut corners. We're proud to be a locally owned business that Dallas trusts.
