Our story

Dallas's Trusted Electricians Since 2009

To provide safe, reliable, and professional electrical services that exceed customer expectations, with transparent pricing and a commitment to quality.

Electrics Contractor Pros was founded in 2009 by a team of master electricians who saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in the Dallas area. Starting with just a single truck, we built a reputation for reliability and craftsmanship, one job at a time. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service electrical contractor serving thousands of satisfied customers across Dallas and surrounding communities.

Today, we continue that tradition of excellence. Every member of our team is a licensed electrician who takes pride in their work. We invest in ongoing training and the latest tools to deliver superior results. Whether it's a simple outlet repair or a complex commercial installation, we treat every project with the same dedication to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction.
